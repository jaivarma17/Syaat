1888
Yogeshwar Ramlal Bhagwan

Mahaprabhu Ji was the First Guru Gaddi and the real inceptor of our purest mission in 1888. His innovative soul, incarnated in India, truly relieved mental and physical stresses of all. With his knowledge of the hidden secrets of Yoga, Mahaprabhu Ji introduced the world to the joy of Yogic practices. For the aspirants of yoga, he is still revered as an ideal soul—a real incarnation of God in Kalyuga—who came to earth to bestow blissful life to every person in every home. Mahaprabhu Ramlal Bhagwan’s divine touch attracted many devotees to the Dhyan Samadhi, which ultimately led to self-realization.

1938
Yogeshwar Mulakhraj Bhagwan

In search of the Yogeshwar, Mulakh Raj Ji met Mahaprabhu Ji and became his devotee. Known for his spiritual miracles, Mulakh Ji was able to perform every possible activity in the state of meditation. From 1938, he led the organization as its second Guru Gaddi and empowered all with a new view towards Yoga. Yogeshwar Mulakhraj Bhagwan’s command on meditation and concentration kriyas made our organization the pioneer of Yog World during his era. In 1951, he declared Yogeshwar DeviDayal Mahadev as the third Guru and his successor. Till 1960, he kept working to teach the Yogic exercise and practices to the world in order to accomplish his sole dream of making yoga a worldwide phenomenon.

1951
Yogeshwar DeviDayal Mahadev

He was the third Guru of our organization, taking charge in 1960. He gave a formal structure to our mission by establishing 17 state-level registered bodies, which are currently running over 70 Yoga temples across the Indian subcontinent. He seamlessly blended his Yogic techniques with more modern yoga programs, which are much appreciated by Yoga followers worldwide. He was a pioneer in this field at the time, as no other organization was solely devoted to the teaching of Yoga and bringing spiritual and physical benefits to the people. His state-of-the-art, customized programs aimed at curing specific physical and mental ailments continue to benefit Yoga followers today.

1998
Yogeshwar Surender Dev Mahadev

From 1998 to 2016, Swami Surender Dev Ji Mahadev worked tirelessly to teach the path of divine lifestyle with higher spiritual goals to his followers. Under his guidance, more than two thousand teachers/Yogacharyas were trained to teach yoga. They are now carrying forward his legacy and teach authentic Yoga practices at 75+ Divya Yog Mandirs/temples. Being the Pradhan Yogacharya Dev of the organization, he introduced the teachings of Yogeshwar Ramlal Bhagwan through his efforts. The first temple of Mahaprabhu Ji was also established under Yogeshwar Surender Dev’s guidance. His pure and saintly nature was wholly devoted to the selfless service of humanity, leading them onto the path of physical and mental peace. He bid farewell to this world and left for the Dham of Yogeshwar after giving us the strength to carry forward his rich legacy.

2016
Yogacharya Amit Dev

Yogacharya Amit Dev is bringing the elixir of Yoga into the lives of common men with his commendable antediluvian Yoga techniques. He is a leader, mentor, and pathfinder and offers his guidance to attain enlightenment. He is offering his life to serve humanity in all the best possible ways. A firm believer in the power of Yoga, he will show you the ways of life enrichment. He poses a charismatic persona and a humble soul. He has taken the responsibility to spread the allure and power of Vedic Yoga worldwide over his shoulders. He is the successor of the philosophy developed by his forefather Yogeshwar Devi Dayal Maharaj. Taking his legacy forward, Yogacharya Amit Dev is making the world a better place to live in numerous ways.

In our yog ashram, we redefine yoga as a fusion of scientific inquiry and spiritual exploration, transcending mere religious connotations. Guided by the revered Guru Parampara lineage, we embrace the belief that every individual benefits from the wisdom and mentorship ('Guru') in their journey towards achieving holistic wellness and self-realization.
