Welcome to Shri Yog Abhyas Ashram Trust [Regd.], a venerable institution with roots tracing back to 1888, when the First Guru Gaddi Yogeshwar Ramlal Bhagwan laid its foundation. Committed to the timeless principles of Yoga, our non-profit organization has evolved into a beacon of tranquility and wellness, dedicated to advancing and disseminating Yoga practices worldwide.

At the heart of our mission lies a profound belief in the transformative power of Yoga as a holistic remedy for human ailments. Embracing Yoga as a scientific solution, we strive to integrate its principles into the fabric of everyday life. With a vision to establish Yogic colleges and schools for the betterment of society, we tirelessly pursue avenues for research, publication, and education.

Our impact extends far beyond the confines of our ashram, as we actively host camps both within India and abroad, spreading the ethos of Yoga to every corner of the globe. Through these immersive experiences, we guide participants of all ages in the practice of Yogic Kriyas, Asanas, Pranayamas, and Mudras, nurturing not only physical well-being but also inner harmony.

Today, the SYAAT International Yogic Cure and Research Center stands as a testament to our dedication, with 32 locations nationwide serving as hubs of healing and enlightenment. Join us on this journey towards a healthier, more harmonious world, where the wisdom of Yoga illuminates every path.

Yoga for Wellness

Yoga encourages holistic wellness. As such, it does not focus on physical health alone, it also brings about a healthy mind that is self-aware of thoughts and habits.

Yoga for Mindfulness

The benefits of Yoga on the Body, Mind, and Spirit are many. As it is, one of the main areas of focus when practicing Yoga is developing Mindfulness.

Yoga for Spirituality

Yoga helps to unify the three aspects of our being - Body, Mind, and Spirit, with a Spiritual Force or Universal Force.
